## Software and Data Availability

All datasets analyzed in this study are publicly available. The mouse embryo developmental scRNA-seq datasets were obtained from Qiu et al. via the CZ CellxGene Collection (Collection ID: 45d5d2c3-bc28-4814-aed6-0bb6f0e11c82). The processed mouse palate 10x multiome (RNA–ATAC) dataset was obtained from Yan et al. via their public repository https://github.com/fangfang0906/Single_cell_multiome_palate.

The CellPace software package is open-source and available on GitHub at https://github.com/Emad-COMBINE-lab/CellPace-release.
